ATLANTIS PARADISE ISLAND AND DOLPHIN CAY DONATES MEDICAL SUPPLIES TO BAARK

Share |

April 21, 2022

Atlantis Paradise Island and Dolphin Cay is yet again exercising its commitment as a good corporate citizen by doing its part to assist local non-profit organizations by donating much needed supplies to assist in their community work.Dolphin Cay recently donated medical supplies to assist The Bahamas Alliance For Animal

Rights and Kindness (Baark) with their work, as they continue to focus on spaying and neutering of animals to help with animal overpopulation and suffering.

"We want to give a great big thank you to Atlantis particularly Dolphin Cay for this great donation of supplies to Baark for our Mobile Spay and Neuter Clinic" says Baark Volunteer Coordinator Bonnie Young, adding, "Baark has been historically completely a volunteer ran organization and we operate solely on donations. Medical supplies aren't cheap so having them donated to us really saves us a lot when doing the surgeries and allows us to do more for the community."

On hand to present the supplies is Atlantis Veterinarian Dr. Amanda Pinder who expressed excitement to be able to donate these much-needed supplies to assist Baark in their work.
